About Research Trends

This is just a quick experiment using Trove to explore some trends in Australian research by visualising thesis numbers over time.

It's suggestive rather than statistical as some of the date metadata is not really up to the task.

Query options

You can use any search string that works in the simple search box in Trove. This includes some fielded and boolean searching.

Examples:

  • Search in titles only – title:(adelaide)
  • Search for a particular subject – subject:(history)
  • Combine them! – title:(adelaide) AND subject:(history)
